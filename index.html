About Us

Krish's Handicraft Store

Krish is a passionate young entrepreneur from Jaipur, Rajasthan, who is taking his family's rich tradition of handicrafts into the digital world.

As the first in his family to bring their craft online, Krish combines a love for traditional Rajasthani artistry with a modern approach to business.

His store offers an exquisite range of handmade products, each reflecting the culture and craftsmanship of Rajasthan, curated for those who appreciate authenticity and heritage.

Krish is dedicated to sharing his family's story and skills with the world, ensuring that the beauty of Rajasthani handicrafts reaches every corner of the globe.
